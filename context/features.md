## Their Business Portfolio

Lisa and Jeff's track record extends far beyond 1701. They have built and operate multiple successful businesses that demonstrate their ability to execute complex projects across technology, hospitality, and community building.

### ADS (Advanced Drainage Systems) - Enterprise Technology

**How They Met & Their Enterprise Credentials**

Lisa and Jeff first worked together at ADS (Advanced Drainage Systems), where they implemented one of the most successful Salesforce solutions in the Aerospace and Defense industry. This enterprise-scale project established their reputation for delivering complex technology solutions.

**The Werby Tracker: Where It All Started**

Before the full Salesforce implementation, Jeff built a prototype CRM called the "Werby Tracker" - a proof-of-concept that demonstrated the business case for a comprehensive CRM solution. Built on a custom WordPress framework with jQuery, the system included reports, dashboards, and email alerts.

The results spoke for themselves: **in less than 3 months, over 20,000 sales-related activities were recorded in the system.** This adoption rate proved to leadership that the sales team needed and would use a proper CRM - paving the way for one of the most successful Salesforce deployments in the Aerospace and Defense sector.

This approach - build a scrappy prototype, prove the value with real data, then scale to an enterprise solution - reflects how Lisa and Jeff operate: practical, evidence-based, and focused on results.

Their team at **Port & Starboard** now provides solutions across multiple domains:

**Technology Services:**
- **Salesforce Implementation & Consulting** - Enterprise CRM solutions with proven success in highly regulated industries
- **Web Development** - Custom web applications and platforms
- **Mobile Application Development** - iOS and Android solutions

**Industries Served:**
- **Aerospace & Defense** - Where they built their reputation with one of the industry's most successful Salesforce implementations
- **Financial Services** - Complex compliance and workflow solutions
- **Economic Development Agencies** - A major focus area, helping regions attract and retain businesses

The ADS Salesforce implementation remains a benchmark in the Aerospace and Defense sector, demonstrating Lisa and Jeff's ability to deliver mission-critical enterprise solutions.

## The Arrival Concept

### Core Vision
A synergistic ecosystem of innovation, arts, and community at Virginia Beach's gateway to the Oceanfront Resort District. A "Metroburb" inspired by the Bell Works model - an urban hub within suburbia where creativity and commerce collide.

### Tagline
**"Ideas Make Landfall at Arrival"**

### Proposed Location
2200 Parks Avenue (former MOCA building)
- 37,829 square feet
- 9.6 acres
- Gateway to Oceanfront Resort District
- Adjacent to ViBe Creative District

## Core Value Propositions

### 1. Innovation & Coworking Center
- **What it is**: Flexible workspace for entrepreneurs, remote workers, and small businesses
- **Why it matters**: Retains talent in Virginia Beach instead of losing them to DC, Richmond, or remote-only work
- **Proven model**: 1701 demonstrated demand exists in Virginia Beach for quality coworking

### 2. Arts and Cultural Galleries
- **What it is**: Exhibition space for visual arts, rotating shows, and cultural programming
- **Why it matters**: Fills the void left by MOCA's departure; supports local artist community
- **Partner**: Creative Well Arts Foundation

### 3. Art Studios & Classrooms
- **What it is**: Working studio space for artists and art education programming
- **Why it matters**: Creates infrastructure for arts community; generates programming revenue
- **Model**: Revenue from classes, workshops, and studio rentals

### 4. Culinary Incubator & Cafe
- **What it is**: Commercial kitchen for food entrepreneurs plus public cafe
- **Why it matters**: Supports food startups; creates anchor amenity for the space
- **Partner**: Port & Starboard, The Crop Foundation

### 5. Events and Performance Venue
- **What it is**: 300-seat auditorium for concerts, film screenings, lectures, pitch nights
- **Why it matters**: Year-round programming draws visitors; diversifies revenue
- **Demand**: Live events ranked #1 in community survey (68.3%)

### 6. Wellness Studio
- **What it is**: Flexible space for fitness classes, yoga, meditation
- **Why it matters**: Health/wellness amenity for members and public
- **Partner**: Oasis Health Group

### 7. Botanical Garden & Outdoor Space
- **What it is**: Sustainable landscape connecting interior to 9.6-acre grounds
- **Why it matters**: Activates outdoor space; environmental education; event venue
- **Partner**: Lynnhaven River NOW

### 8. Tech Lab & Podcasting Studio
- **What it is**: Technology resources including podcast studio, maker space
- **Why it matters**: Supports tech entrepreneurs and content creators
- **Model**: Revenue from studio rental, tech programming

## Competitive Differentiators

### vs. Traditional Coworking (WeWork, Regus, etc.)
- **Community focus**: Not just desks, but programming and connection
- **Arts integration**: Unique combination of workspace and cultural venue
- **Local ownership**: Profits stay in Virginia Beach
- **Proven operators**: Lisa and Jeff have done this before successfully

### vs. Traditional Arts Venue
- **Sustainable model**: Revenue-generating programs, not donation-dependent
- **Multi-use**: Not just exhibition but creation, education, and events
- **Year-round activation**: Coworking ensures daily foot traffic

### vs. City-Operated Facility
- **Entrepreneurial management**: Faster, more flexible decision-making
- **Private capital**: Reduces burden on city budget
- **Business expertise**: Team with startup and corporate experience

## Content Creation Guidelines

When writing about Arrival's features:

1. **Lead with community benefit**: "Arrival would give Virginia Beach entrepreneurs a home" not "Arrival has coworking space"
2. **Reference 1701 precedent**: Show that this model already worked in Virginia Beach
3. **Cite the survey data**: 86% approval is a powerful proof point
4. **Name the partners**: Lynnhaven River NOW, Creative Well, etc. add credibility
5. **Include financials**: $12M/5 years shows viability, not wishful thinking
6. **Connect to city goals**: Resort Strategic Growth Area, Central Beach Gateway
7. **Acknowledge what happened**: The RFI was canceled - be transparent about that
