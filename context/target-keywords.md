# Target Keywords & Topic Clusters for Arrival VB

This document organizes Arrival's target keywords by topic cluster for strategic content planning and SEO optimization. Given the unique situation (RFI canceled, building awareness and seeking partnerships), our keyword strategy focuses on local visibility, credibility building, and community development topics.

---

## Topic Cluster Structure

Each topic cluster includes:
- **Pillar Keyword**: Main, primary topic
- **Cluster Keywords**: Related subtopics
- **Long-Tail Keywords**: Specific, lower-competition phrases
- **Search Intent**: What users want
- **Content Priority**: How important this cluster is for our goals
