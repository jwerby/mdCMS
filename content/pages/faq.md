# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Arrival?

Arrival is a Virginia Beach-based business growth partner offering consulting, recruiting, investing, and accelerator services to help local businesses scale and succeed.

### Who do you work with?

We work with businesses at all stages - from early-stage startups to established companies looking to scale. Our focus is on the Virginia Beach and Hampton Roads region, though we serve clients throughout the Mid-Atlantic.

### How do I get started?

The first step is a discovery call where we learn about your business, challenges, and goals. From there, we'll recommend the services that best fit your needs.

## Consulting Questions

### What types of consulting do you offer?

Our consulting services cover strategy, operations, digital transformation, and market expansion. We tailor our approach to your specific business challenges and objectives.

### How long are typical consulting engagements?

Engagements range from focused 4-week sprints to ongoing advisory relationships. The duration depends on the scope and complexity of your needs.

### Do you work with startups or only established businesses?

Both! We have experience helping early-stage startups find product-market fit as well as helping established businesses optimize operations and enter new markets.

## Recruiting Questions

### What positions do you recruit for?

We specialize in executive search, technical recruiting, and key strategic hires. We focus on positions where the right person can significantly impact business outcomes.

### How is your recruiting different from traditional agencies?

We take a consultative approach, understanding your business strategy and culture before beginning any search. This results in better long-term fits and lower turnover.

### What industries do you have experience recruiting for?

Our experience spans technology, professional services, manufacturing, healthcare, and government contracting - the key industries in the Hampton Roads region.

## Investing Questions

### What stage companies do you invest in?

We primarily invest in seed and early growth stage companies in the Virginia Beach area. We look for strong teams, clear market opportunities, and sustainable business models.

### How much do you typically invest?

Investment amounts vary based on the opportunity and stage. We're flexible and often co-invest with other local and regional investors.

### What do you look for in an investment?

Beyond financials, we look for passionate founders, scalable business models, and opportunities where we can add value beyond capital through our network and expertise.

## Accelerator Questions

### How long is the accelerator program?

Our core accelerator program runs 12 weeks, with options for continued support and mentorship after the program concludes.

### Is the accelerator program only for tech companies?

No, we accept companies from various industries. The key criteria are growth potential, coachable founders, and a commitment to the program.

### Do I need to be full-time to participate?

We strongly recommend that founders are full-time committed to their business during the accelerator. The program is intensive and requires significant time investment.

### Is there a cost to participate?

Program structure varies. Contact us to learn about current cohort opportunities and terms.
